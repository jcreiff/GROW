Partner with us

Ready to see some sprouts of hope and resilience in your language group or community? Come and GROW this global project with us!

We believe that people everywhere need spiritual and emotional tools for resilience and that the best way to make those tools available is through teamwork. The world is big. The needs are great. And yet grassroots projects spread this way. Join our team!

Pray With Us

Join our growing group of prayer partners and receive updates on how you can join us in the FIRST STEP - prayer!

Sign up by sending us an email at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com

Research

GROW curriculum programs are implemented through evidence-based research and outcomes. Our work is never finished. If you are a researcher interested in this topic, we would love to hear your thoughts and talk with you! Contact us today at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.

Volunteer

Do you have an interest in serving with our team in the U.S. or abroad? We would love to hear from you! Reach out to us at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.

Give

As a non-profit organization, we rely on the support of generous donors and grant funding. If you are interested in our work and would like to contribute to GROW, we would love to share with you how you can donate! Contact us today at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.

Translate Our Curriculum

As a global organization, many of our participants, leaders, and volunteers speak English as a second language. Would you volunteer to translate the GROW curriculum for your language group? Would you like to help? Let us know by sending an email to globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.

Lead a Group

Want to know more about how you can bring GROW to your country? Let’s talk!
Email us at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.

Serve as an Ambassador

GROW ambassadors are men and women who are willing to serve by acting as connectors. They recruit people in their community to be GROW leaders. They organize these volunteers and help them get online training. For more information contact us at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.

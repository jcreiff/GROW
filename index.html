Resilience-Based Recovery Program
Our sister organization “Celebration of Liberation” is now piloting a resilience based alcohol and drug recovery program in Zambia, South Africa and Kenya. Celebration of Liberation uses Bible stories to teach ten powerful character practices that help people build a life of inner strength as they overcome alcohol and drug problems.

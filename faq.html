Frequently Asked Questions

Can the GROW program be used with people of all ages?
Yes! The GROW program has been piloted with children as young as 10 and adults as old as 82. It connects with people of all stages of life!

Who can participate in GROW?
Everyone is welcome! We welcome people from all different faiths, cultures and language backgrounds to participate in the GROW program. The need for resilience and strong character is universal!

Has GROW been translated into other languages?
We are currently working on translations in Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, Korean, French, and German.

How can I access the curriculum?
The curriculum will be released online by the spring of 2020. Check back in with us!

Is GROW copyrighted?
Yes. However, we are currently working to allow you to download and make copies of the curriculum.

How can I make a donation?
If you are interested in making a tax-deductible donation to GROW, please contact us at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com. We welcome any donation!

What is the best way to contact you?
Email! Please email us at globalresilienceoralworkshops@gmail.com.
